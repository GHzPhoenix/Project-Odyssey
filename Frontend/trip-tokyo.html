7-Day Tokyo Discovery

Immerse yourself in the vibrant city life of Tokyo, where tradition meets technology. This 7-day Tokyo Discovery includes everything you need to experience the best of Japan’s capital—from sushi and shrines to neon lights and nature.

Duration 7 Days / 6 Nights
Location Tokyo, Japan
Includes 4-star hotel, daily breakfast, Tokyo city tour, Mt. Fuji day trip, airport transfer
Price From €999 per person

Itinerary Highlights

  • Day 1: Arrival in Tokyo – Transfer to Hotel
  • Day 2: Tokyo City Tour – Asakusa, Shibuya, Shinjuku
  • Day 3: Full-Day Mt. Fuji and Lake Ashi Tour
  • Day 4: Free Day – Optional Disney Resort or Ghibli Museum
  • Day 5: Traditional Tea Ceremony & Ueno Park Visit
  • Day 6: Shopping at Harajuku and Omotesando
  • Day 7: Departure
