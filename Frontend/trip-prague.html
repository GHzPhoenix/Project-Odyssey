4-Night Prague Cultural Escape

Explore the charming city of Prague with its historic Old Town, majestic castles, and vibrant culture. This 5-day journey offers an immersive look at Czech history and gastronomy.

Duration 5 Days / 4 Nights
Location Prague, Czech Republic
Includes 4-star hotel, city pass, walking tour, local cuisine dinner
Price From €590 per person

Itinerary Highlights

  • Day 1: Arrival and welcome dinner in Old Town
  • Day 2: Guided tour of Prague Castle and Charles Bridge
  • Day 3: Museum visits and cultural evening
  • Day 4: Boat ride on Vltava River and shopping
  • Day 5: Breakfast & Departure
