7-Day Swiss Alps Getaway

Experience breathtaking views, charming alpine villages, and thrilling mountain adventures in the heart of Switzerland. This 7-day package includes everything you need to escape into the snow-covered beauty of the Alps.

Duration 7 Days / 6 Nights
Location Swiss Alps – Interlaken, Zermatt, Lucerne
Includes Hotel stay, ski pass, train pass, guided hikes, daily breakfast
Price From €899 per person

Itinerary Highlights

  • Day 1: Arrival in Zurich & train to Interlaken
  • Day 2-3: Skiing & hiking in the Jungfrau region
  • Day 4: Scenic train ride to Zermatt
  • Day 5: Visit to Matterhorn & Gornergrat Bahn
  • Day 6: Explore Lucerne’s Old Town
  • Day 7: Return to Zurich and departure
